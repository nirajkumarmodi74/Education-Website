SCM VS MBA

SCM (Supply Chain Management) and MBA (Master of Business Administration) are both valuable career paths, but they focus on different areas of business and management.

SCM is more specialized and focuses on the flow of goods, services, and information across supply chains. It is ideal for students interested in logistics, operations, and process optimization.

MBA is a broader degree that covers various aspects of business such as finance, marketing, human resources, and leadership. It is suitable for those who want to explore multiple management roles or move into leadership positions.
